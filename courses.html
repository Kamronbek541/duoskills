About this course

The Oxford Artificial Intelligence Programme is a cutting-edge, six-week online course offered by University of Oxford. Designed for business leaders, managers, and professionals, this program provides an in-depth understanding of artificial intelligence, its practical applications, and its ethical considerations.

  Through expert-led modules, participants explore the history and evolution of AI, delve into machine learning and deep learning concepts, and learn how to implement AI-driven strategies in their organizations. The course emphasizes real-world applications and equips learners with the knowledge to critically assess the societal and ethical implications of AI technology.

No prior coding experience is required, making it accessible to a diverse audience. By the end of the course, participants will have the skills to identify AI opportunities, develop a business case for AI, and lead initiatives to integrate AI into their professional environments effectively. Upon successful completion, learners will receive a certificate of attendance from Oxford, solidifying their expertise in this transformative field.

Curriculum

1. Artificial Intelligence Ecosystem:

Explore the history and potential of AI within the digital ecosystem.

2. AI and Machine Learning:

Understanding the Black Box: Delve into the mechanics of supervised, reinforcement, and unsupervised learning.

3. Understanding Deep Learning and Neural Networks:

Gain insights into deep learning and its role in modern AI.

4. Working with Intelligent Machines:

Examine AI's impact on the labor market and human-machine interaction.

5. The Ethics of Artificial Intelligence:

Investigate ethical and legal considerations surrounding AI.

6. How to Drive AI in Your Business:

Identify business opportunities for AI implementation

Who should Enroll

Managers and Business Leaders:

Seeking to understand AI's workings, possibilities, and ethical implications

Technical Professionals:

Such as CIOs, IT managers, and business analysts aiming to implement AI within their organizations.

Outcomes:

Develop an informed opinion on AI's applications and implications.

Build a business case for AI implementation in your organizational context.

Critically examine the social and ethical implications of AI.

Gain a conceptual understanding of machine learning algorithms, deep learning, and neural networks.
